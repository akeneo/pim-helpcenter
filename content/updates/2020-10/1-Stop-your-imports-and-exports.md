# Stop your imports and exports
::: meta-data type="New" features="Productivity" available="late October" link-to-doc="../articles/TODO.html"
:::

A brand new `Stop` button is now displayed in your process tracker. It enables you to stop the Excel and CSV imports/exports you launched. Super helpful whenever you launch a job by mistake and don't want to wait for it to execute until the end. ðŸ˜‰

![The new `Stop` button in the process tracker](../img/TODO.png)
